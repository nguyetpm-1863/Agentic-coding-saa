## 1. Screen Overview

The "Dropdown Phong ban" is a dropdown list component that allows users to select a department to filter content on the page. When expanded, it shows a vertical list of department names on a dark background. The currently selected department ('CEVC2') is highlighted with a lighter background. Selecting a department applies a page-wide filter to show kudos for people belonging to that department.

## 5. Functional Behavior

- **Click on item:** Selects department, closes dropdown, applies page-wide filter (filters kudos for people in that department)
- **Hover on item:** Displays subtle highlight effect, pointer cursor
- **Selected state:** Active/selected item displays highlight (lighter background)
- **Single selection:** Only one department can be selected at a time
- **Scroll:** Dropdown shows scrollbar when items exceed visible area (50 total departments, only ~6 visible at once)
