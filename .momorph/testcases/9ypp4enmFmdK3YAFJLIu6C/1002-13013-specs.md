## 1. Screen Overview

The "Dropdown list hashtag" is a dropdown component that allows users to select hashtags from a predefined list. Users can select up to a maximum of 5 hashtags. The dropdown has a dark-themed background and displays hashtag items with a '#' prefix. Selected items show a golden checkmark icon on the right side. The component header displays "Hashtag" with a "+" icon and subtitle "Toi da 5" (Max 5).

## 4. Functional Rules

- **Selection:** Click on a hashtag item to toggle select/deselect
- **Maximum selection:** 5 hashtags. When 5 are selected, further selection is blocked (disabled)
- **Selected state:** Check icon appears on the right side of the item
- **Unselected state:** Check icon is hidden, space is preserved
- **Hover effect:** Background color changes slightly to highlight the item

## 5. Visual Specifications

- Dropdown background: Dark/black theme
- Header: "Hashtag" label with "+" icon and "Toi da 5" subtitle
- Selected items: Golden/yellow checkmark icon (24x24px) on the right
- Unselected items: No checkmark, white text on dark background
- Hashtag text: White text with '#' prefix
- Hover state: Slight background color change
