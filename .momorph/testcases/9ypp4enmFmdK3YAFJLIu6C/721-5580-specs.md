## 1. Screen Overview

The "Dropdown Hashtag filter" is a dropdown list component used to filter content by hashtag. It displays a vertical list of hashtag items on a dark background. One item can be selected at a time, shown with a glow/highlight effect. Clicking an item selects it, closes the dropdown, and applies the hashtag filter to the entire page. The list scrolls when items exceed the visible area.
